Tiebeam Branding
and Web Design  

Tiebeam Cider House is a rustic restaurant that focuses primarily on cider and barbecued food. The identity aims to portray the rustic feel with the use of wood beams in the “top icon.” The chosen colours are recognizable and basic rustic colours. The word “Tie beam” comes from the world of timber framing. At the cider house, flights of cider are served on wooden planks, similar to the ones used for tie beams.
