Who Am I?

My name is Aaron Morrison.

I am a multi-media designer who is passionate about sports and brand design. I have previously worked with Conestoga Varsity teams to create engaging social media graphics, and VALA Skincare Company for advertising. I have done many promotion and recruiting projects for up-and-coming athletes. Most recently, I created an official recruitment post for the Rochester Institute of Technology (RIT) Men’s lacrosse team.

Along with designing for others, I recently launched golf apparel brand “Beached Golf.” The business found immediate success with products in local golf shops and professional golf ambassadors across North America.

I am passionate about a wide range of design and brand creation.
