Swift Catalogue

This catalogue, transit ad and business card was created for a Electric Vehicle Expo called "Swift EV Expo." The expo was created to attract innovative beings with the goal of sharing the growing world of electric transportation options. The catalogue highlights the most popular option fo the kind. In addition to the catalogue, I created business cards and a transit advertisement for the event.

This project was in collaboration with Shelby Luis-Spieser
